Help Us Understand the Link Between Hearing and Alzheimer’s

If you’re 60+ and don’t wear a hearing aid, you could earn £200 by volunteering in safe NHS research.

Call Now Email Us

Who Can Volunteer?

  • Age 60+

  • Do NOT wear a hearing aid

Why Volunteer?

  • Receive a payment of up to £200

  • Contribute to NHS research into the link between hearing and Alzheimer’s

  • Support future generations

What’s Involved?

  • Total time: 3–4 hours

  • 1.5h screening visit with NHS team

  • 1.5h brainwave test (listening to sounds + watching a silent movie)

  • Non-invasive & completely safe
